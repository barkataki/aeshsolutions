Overview

Aesh Solutions ("Aesh," "we," "us," or "our") provides SAP AI consulting services from New Jersey, USA. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website and consulting engagements, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

This is a general template provided for the website and is not legal advice. You should have it reviewed by qualified counsel before relying on it.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. When you submit our contact form or email us, we collect your name, company, email address, phone number, and the contents of your message.
  • Engagement information. During consulting work we may process project materials and data you share with us under a separate agreement.
  • Usage information. Basic, aggregated analytics such as pages visited and approximate location, collected to improve the site. We do not use this to identify you personally.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services;
  • To deliver, maintain, and improve our consulting engagements;
  • To send relevant communications you have requested;
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information.

How we share information

We share information only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, our form provider and email host), when required by law, or in connection with a business transfer. All providers are bound by confidentiality obligations. Contact-form submissions on this site are processed by Formspree on our behalf.

Cookies & analytics

We use two categories of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential cookies. Required for the Site to function (for example, remembering your cookie choice). These are always active and do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies. Used only after you accept them via our cookie banner. They help us understand aggregated, non-identifying traffic patterns so we can improve the Site.

When you first visit, our banner lets you Accept all or Decline non-essential cookies; we store your choice locally and load analytics only if you accept. You can change your decision at any time by clearing your browser's site data, which will prompt the banner again. We honor browser "Global Privacy Control" (GPC) signals where required by law.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures — including access controls, encryption in transit, and audit trails — to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

California privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the CPRA, gives you the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose;
  • Access and request a copy of your personal information;
  • Delete personal information we hold about you, subject to legal exceptions;
  • Correct inaccurate personal information;
  • Opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" of personal information; and
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CPRA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our contact page; we will verify and respond within the timeframes required by law. You may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf.

EEA & UK privacy rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (and UK GDPR) apply to our processing of your personal data.

Legal bases. We process personal data on the basis of your consent (for analytics cookies and marketing), our legitimate interests (to operate and improve the Site and respond to inquiries), performance of a contract (to deliver services you request), and compliance with legal obligations.

Your rights. You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing. Where we rely on legitimate interests, you may object to that processing.

  • Data controller. Aesh Solutions, New Jersey, USA.
  • International transfers. Data may be processed in the United States; where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Retention. We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described, then delete or anonymize it.
  • Complaints. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our contact page.

Questions about this policy? Reach us through our contact page. We are based in New Jersey, USA.
