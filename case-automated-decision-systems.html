Automated decision systems for Accounts Payable

How Aesh helped a major US water utility streamline AP operations with AI-powered Vendor Invoice Management from OpenText — turning a manual, multi-day process into touchless, decision-ready automation.

Industry
Utility — water services
Scale
1 billion gallons of water daily to 14 million people across 14 states
Workforce
7,000 employees
Featured solutions
Vendor Invoice Management · Invoice Capture Center

Accounts Payable was slow, manual, and opaque. We deployed OpenText Vendor Invoice Management on SAP S/4HANA — embedding AI-powered capture, intelligent routing, and automated approval workflows so invoices move from receipt to posting with minimal human touch.

Before · The challenge

Manual AP processing was slowing cash flow and straining suppliers

  • Invoice processing cycle times of 5 days or more, driven by manual data entry, validation, and approval.
  • Only 30% of scanned invoices flowed straight through without manual intervention.
  • Longer payment cycles and growing supplier frustration over invoice status.

The solution

Vendor Invoice Management — receipt to posting, automated

OpenText VIM, integrated with SAP S/4HANA, automates each stage of the invoice lifecycle. AI-powered OCR and document information extraction capture invoice data; intelligent routing and automated workflows handle approvals and exceptions; a Fiori app gives the team real-time visibility.

Why OpenText

  • Vendor Invoice Management automates invoice processing from receipt to posting — no manual intervention except defined exceptions.
  • Invoice Capture Center uses AI-powered OCR, intelligent routing, and automated escalation to handle invoice exceptions.
  • A Fiori app gives teams quick, real-time visibility into invoice status.
  • Seamless integration with SAP ERP and S/4HANA.
